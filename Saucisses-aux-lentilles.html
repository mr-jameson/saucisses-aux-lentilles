Saucisses aux Lentilles

Saucisses aux Lentilles, or Lentils with Sausages in English, is a simple French dish great for a winter nighti-in with a glass of red. A fresh baguette with salted butter and a salad provide the perfect complement to the meal.

Ingredients

  • 6 large gourmet pork sausages
  • 1 cup dried green lentils
  • 1 large brown onion
  • 2 carrots
  • 1 litre of beef stock
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 bunch of fresh thyme
  • A squeeze of lemon juice to taste

Method

  1. In a heavy pot, add the olive oil and brown the sausages on medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onions and carrots and cook until golden brown.
  3. Add the dried lentils to the pot and stir until they are coated in oil.
  4. Mix 500 ml of stock, bay leaves and fresh thyme. Bring to the boil.
  5. Cook on low heat for 40 - 60 minutes, until lentils are soft and tender. Add more stock if needed to give a thin gravy consistency.
  6. Season with salt, pepper and lemom juice. Serve with a french baguette to mop up the juices.
